NFID is a Web3 wallet protocol.
- As a digital asset holder, NFID lets you store, buy, send, receive, swap tokens and collect NFTs without complex apps, plugins, or seed phrases.
- As a user of internet applications, NFID lets you create accounts without usernames or passwords and keeps your identity private between apps.
- As an NFID holder, all personal data submitted to any application is owned by you and shareable across any other app.

Explore our guides and examples to use NFID, and for developers to integrate it into your applications.
