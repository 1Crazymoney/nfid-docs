## What is NFID?
NFID Wallet is a chain-agnostic smart contract wallet that integrates with your application to enable passwordless Web3 onboarding and authentication.</br></br>
NFID Wallet+ is a Web3 Wallet Acquisition Platform to acquire more engaged wallets, save application data, and request verified identity data.</br></br>
Whether you're integrating the base Wallet or premium acquisition tools with Wallet+, your onboarding rates are guaranteed to increase.
